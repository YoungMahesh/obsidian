## Outbound firewall

#### `ufw` vs `iptables`
`iptables` is the native Linux firewall tool that directly interacts with the kernel’s netfilter system.
`ufw` simplifies firewall management by translating simple commands into iptables rules.
`ufw` (Uncomplicated Firewall) = a user-friendly wrapper built on top of `iptables`
