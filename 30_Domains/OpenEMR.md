
**OpenEMR** is a free, open-source **Electronic Medical Record (EMR)** and **Electronic Health Record (EHR)** system used by clinics, hospitals, and healthcare providers to manage patient data and operations.

It’s web-based and can be self-hosted (on your own server) or deployed in the cloud.

---

## 🔹 What OpenEMR Does

### 1️⃣ Patient Management

* Store patient demographics
* Medical history
* Allergies, vitals, diagnoses
* Attach lab reports & documents

### 2️⃣ Clinical Workflows

* Encounter notes
* Prescriptions (eRx support)
* SOAP notes
* Lab & imaging orders
* Clinical decision support

### 3️⃣ Appointment & Scheduling

* Calendar view
* Provider scheduling
* Reminders

### 4️⃣ Billing & Insurance

* Insurance claims (EDI support)
* CPT/ICD coding
* Invoicing
* Payment tracking

### 5️⃣ Standards Support

* **HL7**
* **FHIR (R4 support)**
* HIPAA compliance features
* Multi-language support
